About Us

Our Vision

WeCare-Ng Community Health Centers is an innovative, national leader in Primary health by delivering high quality, integrated care for underserved communities. weCare endorses and promotes The Triple Aim of taking care of the health needs of the patient, improving the health of the population and at the lowest possible cost. WeCare-Ng will continue to look beyond healthcare to the social determinants of health and will focus on the contributing factors that allow members of the community to lead healthy, fulfilling lives. WeCare-Ng will accomplish this by expanding services to respond to community need, including specialty services, health education and other services. WeCare-Ng places top priority on advocacy, and will continue to be a strong voice for the communities it serves, working at the local and national levels to demand improved healthcare support from local, state and federal government.

About Us

Our Value - WeCare-Ng Community Health Centers are committed to:

  • Providing services with respect and sensitivity to persons of all cultural and socio-economic backgrounds
  • A community partner to provide affordable and accessible quality services.
  • Providing continuity of care through an interdisciplinary approach that meets the needs of our communities.
  • Maintaining a diverse workforce in a professional environment that promotes employee satisfaction and well-being
  • Providing educational and professional growth opportunities for people in the community
  • Providing leadership and advocacy to empower our communities.
  • Operating in well-maintained, attractive facilities with high-quality equipment.
  • Remaining an independent, fiscally sound non-profit organization.
