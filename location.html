Community Engagement

We are proud to support our community through various programs that embody the WeCare-Ng core values of doing what is right and providing value and service. We strongly believe in giving back to the community, which has supported us. Through our philanthropy and volunteerism, we are committed to doing our part to help build viable communities, where our patients and employees live and work. Therefore, we support programs and organizations that help make our communities and estates a better, stronger and healthier place to call home.

We are located in Western Nigeria, with additional medical pavilion planned in the following locations:

  • WeCare-Ng, Isheri - Isheri Medical pavilion building foundation in-progress

  • WeCare-Ng, Shimawa - Shimawa Land acquired and allocated

  • WeCare-Ng, Epe - Epe Land sources in-progress

  • WeCare-Ng, Lekki-Dangota Axis - Land acquired

  • WeCare-Ng, Lekki - Land acquired

  • WeCare-Ng, AgoIwoye - Ago-Iwoye Land acquired and fenced

Is a community healthcare center our mission is to provide care for all irrespective of the their financial capacity.
